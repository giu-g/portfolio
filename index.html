2024 Casa das Antenas Systems Developer and Analyst Intern

In 2024, I worked as a Systems Developer and Analyst Intern at Casa das Antenas, a technology retail store in Limeira, SP. During my time there, I assisted in designing and implementing a new sales database to improve data organization and tracking.

I cleaned and processed over 13,500 sales records, ensuring greater data accuracy and supporting better business insights. By analyzing operations, I identified inefficiencies and helped optimize inventory and supply processes, leading to a 12% reduction in supply waste.
